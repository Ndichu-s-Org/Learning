## **What tells you about direction?**

The **value of the dot product itself** (-7.88) tells you about direction:

| Dot Product Value | What it means |
|-----------------|---------------|
| **Positive** (e.g., 5.2) | Vectors point in **similar** direction (angle < 90°) |
| **Zero** (0) | Vectors are **perpendicular** (angle = 90°) |
| **Negative** (e.g., -7.88) | Vectors point in **opposite** directions (angle > 90°) |
